📷 About Vishwalaksha Smart Camera System

Vishwalaksha is an advanced AI-powered smart camera system designed to act as a vigilant digital sentinel for public and private spaces. Developed with state-of-the-art computer vision and real-time analytics, Vishwalaksha automatically detects suspicious, criminal, and life-threatening activities, alerting authorities instantly to enable rapid response and prevention.

Powered by cutting-edge object detection, behavioral analysis, and AI pattern recognition, the system continuously monitors its surroundings and identifies critical events with high accuracy — without human bias or fatigue.
